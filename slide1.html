Why is calcium important for your health?

Calcium is an important mineral that is needed to:

  • Maintain strong bones
  • Help the movement of muscles
  • Carry nerve messages between the brain and body parts
  • Help blood vessels move blood throughout the body
  • Help release hormones and enzymes that are involved in almost every function in the human body

Source: NIH. Calcium: Fact sheet for consumers. Available at: http://ods.od.nih.gov/factsheets/Calcium-QuickFacts/#h6
